Cobweb lets you stream micropayments from your browser to the websites you visit. For creators, this means you can set a single tag on your website to get paid. For fans, this means you can automatically support your favourite blogs and sites by streaming tips in the background.

Cobweb doesn't break the bank either, like pricy direct subscriptions. Users can choose to allow web monetization for one visit to unlock exclusive content just once. Cobweb helps shift from a patron-based web economy to a content-focused one.

why cobweb?

The conventional Web Monetization ecosystem is currently highly centralized around a single for-profit payment provider, Coil, and a single cryptocurrency and chain, XRP.

Instead, Cobweb is built on the more popular Ethereum cryptocurrency. Cobweb cuts out the middleman tools, making setup much easier for those who already have an Ethereum wallet. Even if you don't have a wallet, getting started is as easy as downloading the user-friendly MetaMask wallet.

For more technical differences, see Cobweb's docs.

how it works

Cobweb scans websites you visit for a Web Monetization tag.

Cobweb connects to your Metamask account to ask for permissions.

Cobweb initiates a payment stream of fractions of a cent with Superfluid.

Payments stream directly to an Ethereum address, no middleman.
