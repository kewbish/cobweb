Cobweb lets you stream micropayments from your browser to the websites you visit. For creators, this means you can set a single tag on your website to get paid. For fans, this means you can automatically support your favourite blogs and sites by streaming tips in the background.

Cobweb doesn't break the bank either, like pricy direct subscriptions. Users can choose to allow web monetization for one visit to unlock exclusive content just once. Cobweb helps shift from a patron-based web economy to a content-focused one.
